# Chapter Close Produces a Meaning Digest and Chapter 2 Hook

## What to build

Make SF2B chapter close produce a compact meaning digest, then use that digest to synthesize the Chapter 2 hook.

The digest should capture what changed and why it matters, not merely summarize what happened. Chapter 2 should open from the consequences of Chapter 1: what the player chose, who noticed, what pressure remained, what was misread, what was promised, and what the next dramatic question should become.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Chapter close produces a structured meaning digest for the completed chapter.
- [ ] The digest includes changed facts, player decisions, promises, NPC/faction movement, unresolved pressures, hard commitments, emotional/genre texture, and what the next chapter must honor.
- [ ] The Chapter 2 Author uses the digest and durable state to create a new hook brief, not a raw transcript continuation or full graph dump.
- [ ] Resolved Chapter 1 objectives do not remain as active Chapter 2 pressure unless intentionally reframed.
- [ ] A playable SF2B Chapter 2 opener can be generated after the Forty Thousand-style Chapter 1.
- [ ] The Chapter 2 opener feels organic and consequential when read without the diagnostics/state view.
