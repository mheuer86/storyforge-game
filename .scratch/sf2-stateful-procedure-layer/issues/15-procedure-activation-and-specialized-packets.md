## What to build

Make the shipped procedure kernel actually govern live play when the fiction enters load-bearing procedure, and ensure the Narrator consumes the specialized procedure packets instead of only a generic list of procedure facts and constraints.

The procedure foundation is present: procedure runtime state, Archivist procedure writes, validation, generic mechanics packets, scene-kernel active procedure ids, and helper derivations for operation, access/exploration, investigation, and combat. But the 2026-05-10 Forty Thousand playthrough never created any `campaign.procedures` records across 14 turns, even while the prose repeatedly revolved around lien clearance, berth release, authorization wires, and departure constraints. The specialized access/exploration helpers also remain mostly fixture/read-side helpers rather than the primary live Narrator surface.

This ticket closes the gap between "kernel is wired" and "kernel affects play."

## Definitions

- **Procedure activation** - state-derived creation or promotion of a procedure runtime because the live fiction now depends on a bounded activity with constraints, affordances, or phase changes.
- **Activation threshold** - procedure state should activate only when it will change future options. Do not create operations just because a genre, seed, or arc could support one.
- **Specialized packet** - the richer access/exploration/investigation/combat derivation, not only the generic `Sf2ProcedurePacket`.
- **Procedure omission drift** - a turn where prose creates repeated procedural blockers while no active procedure or obligation state exists to own them.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Procedure activation heuristics or Archivist guidance create active procedure records when prose introduces load-bearing access, exploration, investigation, combat, or operation constraints.
- [ ] Activation remains optional: social, crew-building, debt, mystery, and fallout chapters do not become operations unless a real procedure is now driving choices.
- [ ] The Narrator receives specialized access/exploration/investigation/combat packets when those procedures are active, not only the generic procedure packet.
- [ ] Procedure omission drift is observable in diagnostics when procedural blockers repeat without an owning procedure or obligation state.
- [ ] Access/exploration specialized packets are integrated into the live scene packet or per-turn delta used by Narrator.
- [ ] Investigation synthesis is integrated when active investigation state or clue density warrants it.
- [ ] Combat procedure packet remains available during active combat and coexists with parent operation/access procedures.
- [ ] A Forty Thousand fixture covers repeated clearance/departure constraints and verifies either a proper access/clearance procedure activates or the system routes the pressure into obligation/thread state instead of free-floating prose.
- [ ] A genre-neutral fixture proves the same activation contract works for a space berth release, a castle gate/writ check, and a cyberpunk subnet credential.
- [ ] Existing procedure helper fixtures still pass.

## Agent Brief

**Category:** bug
**Summary:** Activate procedure state in live play and feed specialized procedure packets to the Narrator.

**Current behavior:** SF2 can persist procedure runtimes and derive specialized access, exploration, investigation, and combat views, but live turns can proceed through repeated procedural constraints with no active procedure owner. Narrator context mostly consumes generic mechanics/procedure summaries rather than the specialized packets.

**Desired behavior:** When a live scene depends on a bounded activity with constraints, affordances, or phase changes, SF2 should either activate the appropriate procedure or route the pressure into another hard-state owner such as obligation/thread state. Active procedures should reach Narrator context through the richest applicable specialized packet.

**Key interfaces:** procedure runtime state, activation heuristics or Archivist guidance, scene packet/mechanics packet construction, specialized access/exploration/investigation/combat packet builders, diagnostics for missing owning state, replay fixture contracts.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] Load-bearing access, exploration, investigation, combat, or operation constraints create or use active hard state.
- [ ] Social, crew, debt, mystery, and fallout scenes remain non-procedural unless a procedure changes future options.
- [ ] Specialized procedure packets are consumed by Narrator context when active.
- [ ] Procedure omission drift is observable in diagnostics.
- [ ] Forty Thousand and genre-neutral fixtures prove repeated blockers gain an owning state record or route to obligation/thread state.
- [ ] Existing procedure helper fixtures still pass.

**Out of scope:** Forcing all chapters into operations, redesigning the whole scene packet, or building new procedure kinds beyond the existing kernel vocabulary.
