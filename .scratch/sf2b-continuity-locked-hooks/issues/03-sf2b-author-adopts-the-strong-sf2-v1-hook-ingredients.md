## What to build

Update the SF2B chapter Author shape so it keeps the best ingredients from the strong SF2 Chapter 2 and the stronger V1 narrator posture.

The Author should create a chapter frame with a social or faction pressure surface, a concrete pressure face, withheld facts, meaningful alternatives, a dramatic objective, and a chapter tension score. It should not collapse back into passive ship procedure just because the player is in transit.

The tension score is the core deliverable: 3-4 active pressure lines with distinct dramatic roles. The Narrator should feel these pressures while writing the scene, but the player should experience them as implication, choice, consequence, and NPC behavior rather than as visible machinery.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] SF2B Author output includes a pressure face or social surface whenever the prior chapter leaves the PC isolated in travel/procedure.
- [ ] Chapter frames include objective, crucible, withheld premise facts, immediate choice, pressure ladder, likely close/reframe vectors, and a tension score.
- [ ] The tension score has 3-4 lines with explicit roles such as foreground objective, relational/social pressure, shadow/faction pressure, and cargo/system pressure.
- [ ] Each tension line identifies its source entity/thread, how it pressures the PC, how it can surface in prose, what advances it, and what would resolve or reframe it.
- [ ] Narrator context receives the current foreground tension and one or two undertow tensions, not the full score as a player-visible checklist.
- [ ] The Author prompt explicitly favors dramatic scene pressure over procedural task queues.
- [ ] A brief V1/SF2 prompt audit is recorded in comments or a scratch note, identifying which narrator/Author instructions were adopted and which were rejected.
- [ ] The Author remains bound by the continuity lock and cannot invent a stronger hook by replacing canon.
- [ ] A fixture or saved Author artifact shows Chapter 2 opening from SF2B Chapter 1 with social/faction pressure, a usable tension score, and exact carry-forward facts.
