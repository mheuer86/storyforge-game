# SF2B Author Prompt Audit

Adopted:
- SF2 continuation strength: pressure face, withheld premise facts, meaningful objective/crucible, social/faction pressure surface, and successor/carry-forward thread discipline.
- V1 narrator strength: pressure should surface through NPC behavior, leverage, implication, choice, and consequence rather than visible machinery.
- SF2B-specific lock: exact carried names, ids, route/cargo facts, contacts, promises, active threads, and closing geometry are hard canon.

Rejected:
- V1-style broad GM improvisation over long transcript memory; SF2B keeps structured state authoritative.
- Pure procedural transit/task queues as a chapter frame when prior continuity leaves the PC isolated in travel.
- Replacing canon with a locally stronger but parallel broker, port, cargo, tail, cutter, or faction pressure.
